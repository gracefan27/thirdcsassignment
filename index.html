About Us

Hi! Welcome to the Kreartsy page! We are a small business that sells calligraphy design prints to anyone through our online website. We are based out of London, Ontario. We started when Grace, the founder of the business, received many requests for her calligraphy artwork, and deciding to make prints of it to spread her love for art and design to the world. She hopes that her artwork brings people as much happiness as it brings her.
